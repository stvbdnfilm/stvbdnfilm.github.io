Development Team

Meet the Team

Our team is a collection of artists who have worked together for years, creating award-winning digital content that has screened locally and abroad. Our focus is on producing narrative content that tells engaging and original stories with a human core.

Stephen Baden

Writer/Director

Stephen Baden is an Edmonton based Writer/Director. He creates and produces web series and short films for Telus, Youtube, and more. He is also Video Production Lead at the marketing agency Lift Interactive, where he creates commercial content for clients such as Lululemon, Tourism Jasper, and more.

Kennedy Bailey

Production Designer

Kennedy Bailey is an Edmonton based filmmaker and designer. While having directed several shorts of her own, Kennedy’s main passion is the design and direction of sets and costumes. Having worked extensively over the years on various projects she brings her exacting eye to ensure that what’s being put into every frame complements and enhances the story and characters.
